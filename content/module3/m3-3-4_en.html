Taxonomy

The classification of information within a specific department is referred to as taxonomy. Taxonomy is a standardized list of terms under which IRBVs are classified. It identifies:

  • What IRBV is being generated by each program or business unit;
  • Where the IRBV is stored; and
  • The type of access granted to the IRBV.

An information classification system using standardized taxonomy:

  • Level 1: Program
  • Level 2: Sub-Program
  • Level 3: Activity
  • Level 4: Sub-activity
  • Level 5: IRBV
