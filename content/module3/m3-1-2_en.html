Types of Classification Systems

Different classification systems represent different approaches to organizing and retrieving information resources. Departments themselves determine which classification system is best suited to their program needs.

Your department may be using one of the following classification systems:

  • Function-based classification
  • Alphabetical classification
  • Numeric classification
  • Block-numeric classification
  • Alpha-numeric classification
  • Hierarchical classification

For a better understanding

Your department would consider the following factors when determining the type of classification tool to use:

  • What is recommended by authorities (TBS, LAC, ISO Standard, etc.);
  • Types of business information systems used across the department;
  • Business context, situation, and needs;
  • Complexity of business activities;
  • Extent of centralization or decentralization of records management responsibilities;
  • Level of support given to users; and
  • Stakeholders.
