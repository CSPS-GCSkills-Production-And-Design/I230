Information Management Common Core

The Information Management Common Core (IMCC) is the baseline for the design and configuration of GCDOCS as an EDRMS solution in the Government of Canada. It provides basic system requirements, a common set of metadata elements, and a standardized taxonomy for common internal services across the government for GCDOCS implementation.

The IMCC contains three parts:

  1. Business rules
  2. Common metadata
  3. Information management architecture
