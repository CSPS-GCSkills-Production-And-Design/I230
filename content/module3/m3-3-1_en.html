For a better understanding

The Government of Canada has adopted a function-based classification approach because:

  • Functions tend to persist within departments; even if a reorganization of the business or reporting responsibility happens, the department’s overall mandate remains the same
  • Government departments are required to provide evidence that they are carrying out their established mandates; it makes sense to organize information resources based on the functions and activities that are specified in that mandate
