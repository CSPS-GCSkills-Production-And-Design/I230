Where to Search

Where you will need to search to find the requested information resource also depends on the type of request being made.

If a client requests a specific physical file (such as a paper contract or an invoice), you would only need to search in the official records office or in the records management system.

.  Which repositories do you need to search in case of an ATIP or investigation request?
Select the correct response.

  Correct! In case of an ATIP, an investigation or a litigation request, you will need to search for information in all formats and in all possible repositories.

  Incorrect. The correct response is D. In case of an ATIP, an investigation or a litigation request, you will need to search for information in all formats and in all possible repositories.
