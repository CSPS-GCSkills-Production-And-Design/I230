Stakeholders

Who is implicated in the search-and-retrieval process will depend on the type of request being made.

In the case of an access to information or privacy request (ATIP), an investigation or a litigation request, stakeholders in the process are:

  • The requesting side (ATIP office, investigation team, litigation team, or legal services)
  • The IM section
  • The program area side (from which information needs to be retrieved)
  • The IT professionals.
