Considerations

Select each section below to learn more about what you need to consider in searching for and retrieving information and data.

  • Search question or topic

    Make sure the question or topic is clear. Make sure you understand it in order to provide the appropriate information or data. Ask the following questions to clarify the search question or the topic:

    • Are there any key synonyms or related keywords which should be used?
    • Are there limitation dates?
    • Are the information or data required in a specific language or format?
  • Originals or copies

    Copies of documents are acceptable to fulfill ATIP and client requests. In legal cases or for the purposes of investigation requests, originals are usually placed on legal hold. You should confirm these points with your legal services or the person in charge of leading the investigation or litigation.
