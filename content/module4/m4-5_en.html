Summary

In this module, you learned about who and what is involved in the search-and-retrieval process as well as some common techniques for finding relevant information resources.

Key points in Module 4

  • Communication and collaboration among stakeholders implicated in the search-and-retrieval process are important right from the start
  • In a litigation case or a special investigation, you will follow the e-discovery process set out by the litigation team or legal services
  • All repositories, databases, and formats should be considered in the search
  • When conducting an advanced search, you can use different operators or wildcards to either narrow or broaden your search results
  • As an IM professional, you support your clients in the search-and-retrieval process to ensure that the business and functional requirements of your department are met

You may proceed to the next module, where you will learn about the retention and disposition of information resources.
