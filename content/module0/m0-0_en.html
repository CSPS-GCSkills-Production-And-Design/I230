Welcome to Course I230 - Information Management: Supporting Programs and Services designed for information management functional specialists.

Information management (IM) supports the effective management of information resources, from planning and systems development to disposal or long-term preservation. It is a key enabler of effective information sharing across departments, with external stakeholders, and with Canadian citizens.

This course was created by the Canada School of Public Service in collaboration with the Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat, Library and Archives Canada, and information management specialists from other Government of Canada departments.
