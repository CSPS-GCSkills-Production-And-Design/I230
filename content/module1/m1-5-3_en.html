Supporting Programs and Services

One of the most important IM services is to provide advice and guidance to program areas on how to properly handle sensitive information and how to maintain and preserve key information resources. This includes:

  • Developing procedures for the maintenance and protection of information resources;
  • Helping program areas understand how to safeguard information against unlawful access, loss, and damage;
  • Delivering awareness sessions on how long information resources should be kept; and
  • Working with program areas to ensure records of archival value are properly preserved.
