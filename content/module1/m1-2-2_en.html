Products and Outcomes

Government employees at all levels collect, create, receive and capture the information resources that are needed to perform their work, make decisions, and document their decisions. The goal is to have departmental and government-wide high-quality information holdings.

The four key outputs from this stage are:

  • information assets
  • appraisal of business value
  • information inventory
  • publications
