Typical Activities

Typical activities at this stage of the records and information life cycle include:

  • Delivering awareness sessions on responsibilities to document business decisions and activities;
  • Assessing information to determine if it has business value or should be considered transitory in nature;
  • Providing advice and guidance on where to save IRBVs; and
  • Building business requirements into your designated corporate repository.
