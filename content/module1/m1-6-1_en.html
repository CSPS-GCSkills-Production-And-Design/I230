Typical Activities

Typical activities at this stage of the records and information life cycle include:

  • identification of IBV that has exhausted their retention periods
  • performing disposition searches in an ERDMS such as GCdocs
  • application of disposition authorities
  • preparation of disposition forms to be signed by offices of primary interest (OPIs)
  • destruction or alienation of information resources
  • preparation of records of archival value for transfer to LAC and documentation of disposition actions

A word from an expert

An important consideration at this stage is to make sure all your disposition processes are being documented. This includes having a disposition plan in place for all information resources, procedures for performing disposition, and ensuring that all disposition authorizations are maintained in a designated corporate repository.
