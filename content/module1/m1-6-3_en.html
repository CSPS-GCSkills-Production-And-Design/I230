Supporting Programs and Services

To support programs and services at this stage of information life-cycle management, your role as an information management functional specialist is to:

  • Deliver awareness sessions to employees on what information resources to keep (IRBVs) and what can be deleted when no longer required (transitory information);
  • Work with program areas in establishing disposition specifications;
  • Liaise with LAC and program areas (OPIs) to develop a records retention and disposition schedule;
  • Ensure that employees are introduced to the proper methods for disposing of information;
  • Provide input on the best methods for documenting disposition actions; and
  • Provide guidance to program areas when transferring records of archival value to LAC.
