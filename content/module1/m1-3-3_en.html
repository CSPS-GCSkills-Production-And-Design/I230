Supporting Programs and Services

Stage 3 enables information sharing across government and ensures program areas can quickly access the information they need to make informed business decisions. By ensuring your department’s information holdings are properly organized, you contribute to openness, transparency, and the security of information in the Government of Canada.

Your role as an information management professional includes:

  • researching various types of organizational tools
  • developing organizational tools such as file plans, classification structures, naming conventions, and metadata schemas
  • helping your department implement organizational tools and apply them to information resources
  • providing guidance, raising awareness among, and training employees with respect to their roles and responsibilities in applying organizational tools
