Supporting Programs and Services

This stage focuses on the use and reuse of information resources and ensures that the foundational pieces are in place for the sharing and release of information and data in a timely fashion. When public servants have timely access to quality information and data, it can only mean the improved delivery of programs and services.

As an IM professional, you need to champion an open-by-default culture in the public service. This means spreading the idea of public servants being stewards of information and data, and not owners of it.

How can you as IM professionals promote and support an open-by-default culture in your department?

Provide your responses in the space below and then compare them with the suggested responses.

Suggested response:

  • Provide advice and guidance on the use, reuse, and sharing of information and data
  • Offer strategic advice on the development, implementation, maintenance, and support of IM tools and systems that enable the effective sharing of information and data
  • Advise end-users on ways to reduce access restrictions
  • Monitor information and data use and dissemination practices
  • Report on irregularities and propose corrective actions
  • Encourage employees to participate in Open Government activities
