You, as an IM professional, should research a variety of information sources when developing your IM plan, including:

  • other governments and jurisdictions, industry, and non-profit and academic communities that are driving global trends in IM
  • government-wide and departmental priorities
  • Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat (TBS) and Library and Archives of Canada (LAC) as central agencies for IM
