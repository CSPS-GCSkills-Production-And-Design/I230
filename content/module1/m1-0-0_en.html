Introduction

This module presents the seven stages of the records and information life cycle, and explains how they support the delivery of the programs and services of the Government of Canada.

What you will learn in this module

  • The importance of each stage.
  • The typical activities at each stage.
  • The key inputs and outputs.
  • Your role as an IM specialist in supporting government programs and services.
