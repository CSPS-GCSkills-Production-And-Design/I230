Supporting Programs and Services

Activities at this stage will allow you to evaluate the IM features in place in your department and to determine which ones need to be improved.

Your role as an information management functional specialist includes:

  • Helping your department to design evaluation methodologies and tools for conducting proper evaluations, environmental scans, and business analyses; and
  • Helping the program areas of your department to write evaluation reports and recommend corrective actions.

.  By helping your department’s program areas with IM evaluation activities, you ensure the accuracy of the department’s information resources and their efficient management and use. True or false?

  Correct. Helping your department’s program areas with IM evaluation activities will ensure that departmental information resources are managed and used in the most efficient way possible. It will also ensure that IRBVs are accurate and that a sufficient level of protection is added to the information resources.

  Incorrect. The correct response is true. Helping your department’s program areas with IM evaluation activities will ensure that departmental information resources are managed and used in the most efficient way possible. It will also ensure that IRBVs are accurate and that a sufficient level of protection is added to the information resources.
