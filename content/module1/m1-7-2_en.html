Products and Outcomes

As an IM professional, you should consider the following key inputs when evaluating an IM program:

  • new or updated Government of Canada IM standards and policies
  • most recent departmental IM plan
  • any findings of the Auditor General of Canada related to IM
  • any findings of special inquiries into IM practices
  • generic or department-specific IM audits, evaluations or client surveys undertaken by LAC
