Why Is This Stage Important?

Stage 7: Evaluation

This stage deals with measuring the performance or progress of your department’s IM program against the predefined criteria outlined in its IM strategy and plan. The goal at this stage is to foster continuous improvement in IM activities within your department.

Evaluating program performance is a key part of the government’s strategy to manage for results. The specific performance goals that were defined in stage 1 (IM Planning) are measured. The results become the catalyst for change and improvement, and are realized in various IM action plans and frameworks.
