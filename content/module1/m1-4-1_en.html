Typical Activities

The typical activities at this stage of the records and information life cycle are as follows:

  • Information is actively accessed, used, and shared by government employees;
  • Barriers to information sharing and reuse are diminished;
  • An open-by-default culture is fostered; and
  • Information is proactively released and published via the Open Government portal.
