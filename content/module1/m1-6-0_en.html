Stage 6: Disposition

This is the stage at which information resources that no longer have business value and have met retention periods can be sent for disposal. Disposition of information resources is a critical component of information life-cycle management. It ensures your department is making decisions based on the most recent and relevant information and it reduces the risks associated with the disclosure of outdated or redundant information.
