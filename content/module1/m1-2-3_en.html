Supporting Programs and Services

This stage ensures public servants have access to accurate and reliable information allowing for evidence-based decision making. Quality information enables the business processes of government to operate more efficiently and effectively.

At this stage, you want to make sure that the IRBVs being collected, created, and received are captured in your department’s designated corporate repository, such as GCDOCS. To this end, you will:

  • Support the chief information officer (CIO) in designating corporate repositories where employees can store IRBVs;
  • Guide, raise awareness among, and train employees on their roles and responsibilities in collecting, creating, receiving and capturing information resources; and
  • Help employees to identify IRBVs.
