Typical Activities

Each IRBV should be subject to a risk profile analysis to ensure it is well maintained, protected, and preserved. Typical activities at this stage of information life-cycle management relate to security and access control, storage of non-electronic resources, and digitization of non-electronic resources.

.  What needs to be considered when performing activities for the maintenance, protection and preservation of records and information? Check all the correct responses.

  Correct. Security and access-control activities include considerations for privacy, security, and confidentiality of information resources. Activities related to the storage of non-electronic resources include considerations for managing hardcopy records and the storage of hardcopy records that contain sensitive information. Activities related to digitization of non-electronic resources include considerations for migrating information resources to other formats if the original format is no longer available.

  Incorrect. All of the above responses are correct. Security and access-control activities include considerations for privacy, security, and confidentiality of information resources. Activities related to the storage of non-electronic resources include considerations for managing hardcopy records and the storage of hardcopy records that contain sensitive information. Activities related to digitization of non-electronic resources include considerations for migrating information resources to other formats if the original format is no longer available.
