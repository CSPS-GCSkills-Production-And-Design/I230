3. Outline how you will get there

A key activity is performing an audit to determine where IM strengths and weaknesses lie.

It is important to provide an accurate picture. Sometimes we see things as we want them to be, and not how they actually are.

Key activities include:

  • meeting with stakeholders and senior management to gain buy-in and support for your IM vision
  • conducting an environmental scan to ensure key information is being considered

Once you have identified the gap between your current state and your desired state, you can plan specific steps. Key activities include:

  • determining long-term objectives and short-term objectives
  • defining action items
  • identifying key performance indicators
  • determining who is the lead for the plan in order to obtain sign off
