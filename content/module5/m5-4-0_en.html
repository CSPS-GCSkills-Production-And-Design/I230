Destruction

The internal destruction of information resources with no business value (transitory information) may take place at the discretion of the employee who collected, received, or created the information.

If an IBV is eligible for destruction, the disposition activities need to be documented to ensure accountability. You, as an information management professional, will complete the disposition activities with input from the managers who are accountable for the IBV.

Where can you undertake electronic disposition?

  • GCdocs or other EDRMS: You need to have a file plan and disposition authority
  • A database: As long as it is covered by a DA and it has the capacity to purge (delete)
  • Well-constructed and controlled shared drive: If the folder reflects a standardized way of classifying information resources (same as a file plan in an EDRMS solution), you would be able to match your folders with a DA
