Managing Information Resources

Retention and disposition processes control the growth and volume of the information resources generated by business activities. Together, retention and disposition systematically ensure that these resources are:

  • Relevant, current, and as complete as necessary
  • Unfettered by resources that are no longer needed for business purposes
