Summary

In this module, you learned about establishing retention specifications and approved disposition methods.

Key points in Module 5

  • Retention specifications are established to facilitate the disposal of information resources once their business use has expired.
  • Designated senior executive for IM, managers, and information management functional specialists collaborate with program areas to play a key role in establishing, implementing, and maintaining retention specifications.
  • Library and Archives Canada identifies information resources of archival value and provides government departments with the authority to dispose of information resources that are no longer required for operational purposes.
  • The internal destruction, transfer to Library and Archives Canada, and alienation or transfer to another governmental department are three approved disposition methods.
