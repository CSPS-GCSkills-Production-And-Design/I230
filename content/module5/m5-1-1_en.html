. Generic Valuation Tools are disposition authorities. True or false?

 Correct! GVTs are not disposition authorities. They do not give Government of Canada departments the authority to dispose of information resources. The authority to dispose continues to be provided through Disposition Authorizations (DAs).

For a better understanding

GVTs help government departments to fulfill their responsibilities relative to the management of information resources. GVTs use various sources of information (such as the TBS profile of internal services, legislation, policies, procedures, industry best practices, and existing business process analysis) and are validated by subject-matter experts of several departments and communities of practice.
