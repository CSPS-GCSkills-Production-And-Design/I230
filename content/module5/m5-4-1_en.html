Destruction Considerations

Information resources may not be destroyed during an access or privacy request proceeding related to that information. Your ATIP Office will generally keep the documents for two years from the date of the last administrative action on file. This does not, however, remove the IM requirements of the original. Once you have sent exact and complete copies of your records to the ATIP Office, the originals may continue to be managed according to its normal life cycle.

. Transitory information is subject to Access to Information and Privacy (ATIP) legislation. True or false?

 Correct! Transitory information, like all other information resources under the care and control of a department, is subject to Access to Information and Privacy (ATIP) legislation.
