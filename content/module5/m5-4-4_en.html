IRBVs may be transferred within the Government of Canada to a different office of primary interest (OPI), business unit or department.

For a better understanding

The departments are typically permitted to transfer or alienate non-archival records without further reference to LAC. Identifying information about the records must be documented. This includes:

  • File number, dates, and format;
  • Retention period and valid DAs;
  • Confirmation of destination and method of transfer of the records; and
  • Any formal agreements regarding the alienation of the records.
