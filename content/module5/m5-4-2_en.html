Transfer to Library and Archives Canada

Records identified by LAC as information resources of archival or historical value, whether textual, electronic or works of art, must be transferred to the care and control of Library and Archives Canada.

LAC provides government departments with guidance on how to prepare records for transfer.

A word from an expert

Archival value is distinct from business value. Not all information resources of business value have archival value. Departments do not have the responsibility of identifying resources of archival or historical value.
