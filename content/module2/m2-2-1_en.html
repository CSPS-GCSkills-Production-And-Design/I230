Controlled Vocabularies

Controlled vocabularies are used to consistently populate metadata fields to ensure the best search results. Many registered controlled vocabularies are used within the Government of Canada to describe different types of information resources.

.  A controlled vocabulary is an established list of nonstandard terms used for both indexing and retrieval of information. True or false?

  Correct! A controlled vocabulary is an established list of standardized terms used for both indexing and retrieval of information. Controlled vocabularies enhance the ability of users to find the information they need when search engines are metadata-enabled.
