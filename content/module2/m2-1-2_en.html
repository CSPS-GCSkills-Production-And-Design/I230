Why Is Metadata Important?

Metadata provides context! The richer the metadata record, the better the information resource is described, making it easier to find and maximizing its use.

For a better understanding

The benefits of using metadata:

  • It provides information on the characteristics and context of the information resource
  • It identifies the value of the information
  • It facilitates the direct discovery of information resources by Canadians
  • It allows for the sharing and exchange of information between government departments and their partners
  • It helps government departments meet their internal information-management needs
  • It supports better decision making

A word from an expert

While some metadata is captured automatically within systems and by devices, all employees can enrich their use of information resources by strengthening or improving their metadata.
